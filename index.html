What is TechConnects

TechConnects is a platform where students and mentors gets involved, invent with their ideas and in process evolve themselves for a bright future.

TechConnects provides a medium for students to submit their ideas irrespective of how small or non-sensical it is. Each idea is protected, reviewed and guided by mentors who have proven themselves with their vast on-field experience.

TechConnects allows student accross geographical area to work together on same idea and colloborate to produce a end product.

TechConnects will help these ideas to patented and market it for business opportunities.
