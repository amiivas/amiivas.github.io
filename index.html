What is TechConnects

TechConnects is a platform where students and mentors gets involved, invent with their ideas and in process evolve themselves for a bright future.

TechConnects provides a medium for students to submit their ideas irrespective of how small or non-sensical it is. Each idea is protected, reviewed and guided by mentors who have proven themselves with their vast on-field experience.

TechConnects allows student accross geographical area to work together on same idea and colloborate to produce a end product.

TechConnects will help these ideas to patented and market it for business opportunities.

Who will get Involved?

WE ALL from the technical sphere of life join hands, as mentors or students to get INVOLVED in this socio-technical platform providing immense opportunities to cater to the technical aptitude of each & every student.

It will provide exposure to first hand experience from the professionals who once crossed the same path & carved their way out to be at some of the envious positions, & connecting to a base of like minded students across the world sharing the same passion towards technological enhancement.

How will you Evolve?

Once involved, the next step in this journey would be a complete transformation of Ideas & Individuals.

Ideas would be guided by subject matter experts to take shape in reality & Individuals would learn in the process immensely Evolving at every step into young professionals ready to take up the challenges, the corporate world has to offer.

What will you Invent?

A well verse says “Necessity is the Mother of Invention”, however, we say “Invention is the Necessity of Today”.

Once the right set of ideas meet the right set of minds, the result is a product which adds value to our own society, something which has benefits in every sphere of life, & the onus lies completely to you, the INVENTOR. Invention is the fruit, the first 2 steps of this journey has to offer.
