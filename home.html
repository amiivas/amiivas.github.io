Title

Concept sketching for designer 3 wheeler cars for Indian roads.

Stream

Mechanical Engineering, Industrial Design

Dates

01/09/2017 Till 31/12/2017

Detail

Cost & space are the 2 major factors that drive the Indian automotive sector. The current design competition aims to disrupt the conventional 4 wheel sector by coming up with designer upscale 3 wheeler cars capable of saving both the cost & space while keeping the design an eye catcher for the mass.

We are looking for concept sketches for the same, the best sketch would be judged by a team of mentors & awarded prizes & recognition.
