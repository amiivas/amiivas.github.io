About Us

Techconnects is a one of its kind socio-technical platform for young engineers to connect with the professional world to serve there technical aptitude, widen there technical approach towards problem solving & enhance their skills right from the beginning to face the very corporate world, giving our society a better & enhanced quality of living, one product at a time.

Techconnects aims to connect the students to a worldwide like minded progressive students base & also to the industry experts (Mentors) who have been in the same position a few years back & have carved there way out into this very corporate professional world.

The vision is to support a student not only with there smallest of technical questions or end year projects but also to put there smallest of ideas onto an innovation platform & develop with them the professional & commercial viability of it. To patent the idea & transforming the unique idea into an invention & the student an Inventor.
